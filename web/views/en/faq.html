Why do I need confirmation in the bitcoin system?

!

When receiving bitcoin coins, the recipient cannot use them immediately. As transfer is made - it is sent to the Bitcoin network for execution and must be included in the block to become validated. The process of incorporating the transfer into block found is called transaction confirmation. Inclusion in 1 block = 1 confirmation, when there are 2 or more such confirmations the transfer is considered confirmed, and the operators, in their turn, process the request. This method was introduced to protect against repeated use of the same bitcoins.
